Research

You have to know the user to make the right decisions, but it doesn't have to be a huge investment. I craft lean research strategies with a mix of methods from my toolbox, including usability studies, user interviews, heatmapping, and multivariate and A/B testing.

Design

Prototyping

I create functional, high fidelity prototypes with HTML and CSS.
